Punctual


"The app that ensures you're always on time."

View on GitHub

Punctual is a reminder app that gives you heads-up notifications on when to leave for an event so that you can arrive punctually. Enter your desired arrival date, destination, and method of transportation; Punctual calculates the travel time and sets up a reminder.

  • Choose how far in advance you’d like your initial reminder.
  • Makes travel estimates for Driving, Walking, Cycling, and Public Transit.
  • Reminders are regularly updated in the background to account for varying travel conditions.
  • Snooze an incoming reminder if you’re not quite ready to leave.
  • Set events to recur (hint: put in your work schedule).

Punctual was developed by Adam Cooper (@AdamBCo) and Nathan Hosselton (@NathanHosselton) as part of their final project for Mobile Makers.
